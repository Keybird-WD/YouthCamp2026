About The Camp

Who Are You
In Christ?

IDENTITY is a three-day youth camp designed to anchor young hearts in the truth of who they are in Jesus Christ. In a world that constantly pressures us to find identity in performance, appearance, and popularity, we return to the only foundation that lasts — the Word of God.

Rooted in Scripture Every session grounded in God's Word, building lasting faith in the next generation.
Spirit-Led Worship & Prayer Encounter God through powerful times of corporate worship and intercession.
Community & Fellowship Build lasting relationships with fellow believers walking the same faith journey.
